The scoring curve for the Digital SAT Practice Test 8 was designed to normalize results across the global testing population. Consistent with typical SAT administrations, the "Heart of Algebra" section carried the most significant weight for the raw-to-scaled conversion.

Recommendation: Focus on foundational algebra accuracy to maximize score gains, as these questions appear most frequently.
