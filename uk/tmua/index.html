About the TMUA

The TMUA tests mathematical thinking and reasoning skills for university admissions. It consists of Paper 1 (Mathematical Thinking) and Paper 2 (Mathematical Reasoning), each 75 minutes with 20 MCQs. From 2024, the test transitioned to a computer-based format (ESAT/TMUA).
