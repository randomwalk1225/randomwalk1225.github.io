Cambridge IGCSE Mathematics

0580 Core & Extended

Cambridge IGCSE Mathematics (0580) is the most widely taken IGCSE worldwide. It offers two tiers: Core (grades C–G) and Extended (grades A*–E). Exams have four papers: Paper 1 (Core short answer), Paper 2 (Extended short answer), Paper 3 (Core structured), Paper 4 (Extended structured). Sessions run in February/March, May/June, and October/November.

Pearson Edexcel International GCSE Mathematics

4MA1 Foundation & Higher

Edexcel IGCSE Mathematics A (4MA1) offers two tiers: Foundation (grades 5–1) and Higher (grades 9–4). The exam consists of two papers per tier, each 2 hours. Main sessions are January and June.

Cambridge vs Edexcel IGCSE

Cambridge (0580) — Most popular internationally; used in 140+ countries. Core/Extended tiers. Three exam sessions per year.
Edexcel (4MA1) — Popular in the UK and internationally; Foundation/Higher tiers. Two sessions per year (Jan & Jun).
