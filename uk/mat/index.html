About the MAT

The Mathematics Admissions Test was used by the University of Oxford (and Imperial, Warwick) for undergraduate maths admissions from 2007 to 2024. It is a 2.5-hour written exam testing problem-solving skills beyond A-Level. From 2026, Oxford uses the TMUA instead. All papers and solutions below are hosted on this site for direct viewing.
