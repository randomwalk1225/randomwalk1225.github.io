About the IB Programme

  • The International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Programme is offered at over 5,000 schools in 159 countries.
  • Math AA (Analysis & Approaches) focuses on rigorous mathematical theory and proof — suitable for students intending to study mathematics or sciences at university.
  • Math AI (Applications & Interpretation) emphasises modelling and real-world applications — suited for business, social sciences, and life sciences.
  • Both courses are offered at Standard Level (SL) and Higher Level (HL). HL chapters are marked above.
  • Read our IB textbook chapters for IB exam strategies and worked past-paper solutions.
