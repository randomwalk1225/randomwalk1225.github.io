About CEMC

  • The Centre for Education in Mathematics and Computing (CEMC) at the University of Waterloo runs Canada's largest math competition program.
  • Over 200,000 students participate in CEMC contests annually from 80+ countries.
  • Contests span Grades 7–12: Gauss → Pascal → Cayley → Fermat → Euclid.
  • All past papers and solutions are freely available from CEMC's official website.
