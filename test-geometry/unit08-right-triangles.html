Quick Reference — Key Formulas

  • Pythagorean Theorem: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$
  • 45–45–90: legs $a$, hypotenuse $a\sqrt{2}$
  • 30–60–90: short leg $a$, long leg $a\sqrt{3}$, hypotenuse $2a$
  • SOH-CAH-TOA: $\sin\theta = \dfrac{\text{opp}}{\text{hyp}}$, $\;\cos\theta = \dfrac{\text{adj}}{\text{hyp}}$, $\;\tan\theta = \dfrac{\text{opp}}{\text{adj}}$
  • Law of Sines: $\dfrac{a}{\sin A} = \dfrac{b}{\sin B} = \dfrac{c}{\sin C}$
  • Law of Cosines: $c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos C$
